# ClawCube Website PRD

## 1. Overview
ClawCube is a hardware product lineup designed for OpenClaw and AI-native workflows. The initial goal is to launch a website that clearly explains the product lineup, communicates positioning, and converts visitors into early customers, leads, or waitlist signups.

## 2. Goal
Build a high-conviction product website that:
- Introduces the ClawCube brand
- Explains the 4 hardware variants clearly
- Helps users choose the right model
- Builds trust around OpenClaw-ready hardware
- Captures purchase intent via contact, inquiry, preorder, or waitlist

## 3. Product Lineup
1. **ClawCube Lite**
   - J4125
   - 8GB RAM
   - Entry-level OpenClaw box

2. **ClawCube Core**
   - Intel N100
   - 16GB RAM
   - Best value general-purpose OpenClaw device

3. **ClawCube NAS**
   - NAS-focused model
   - Personal AI + storage hub

4. **ClawCube Ultra**
   - RTX 4090 48GB-class
   - High-performance AI workstation/server

## 4. Target Users
### Primary
- AI enthusiasts
- Developers building with OpenClaw
- Indie hackers
- Self-hosters / homelab users

### Secondary
- Small teams wanting private AI infrastructure
- Creators and researchers needing local AI systems
- Power users who want turnkey AI hardware

## 5. Core User Problems
- Users don't know what hardware is suitable for OpenClaw
- Existing mini PC / NAS / GPU server options feel fragmented and hard to compare
- Setting up AI-ready hardware is confusing
- Users want a trusted, opinionated hardware recommendation

## 6. Value Proposition
ClawCube offers a curated range of hardware products purpose-built for OpenClaw, from lightweight personal agents to high-performance local AI systems.

## 7. Website Objectives
- Explain what ClawCube is in under 10 seconds
- Present lineup comparison clearly
- Drive qualified leads or preorder intent
- Make ClawCube feel credible, modern, and AI-native

## 8. Key Pages
- Home
- Product lineup overview
- Individual product detail pages
- Use cases
- Why ClawCube / Why OpenClaw-ready
- FAQ
- Contact / Waitlist / Preorder

## 9. Key Features
- Product comparison table
- Product selector / recommendation flow
- Waitlist or inquiry form
- CTA blocks throughout the site
- FAQ for common buying questions
- Optional blog / updates section later

## 10. Success Metrics
- Waitlist signups
- Contact / inquiry submissions
- Product page CTR
- Time on page
- Conversion rate from landing page to lead action

## 11. Open Questions

- Whether the Ultra model is positioned as workstation, server, or both
