# ClawCube Brand Positioning

## One-liner
ClawCube is purpose-built hardware for OpenClaw and self-hosted AI.

## Positioning Statement
For developers, AI enthusiasts, and self-hosters who want reliable AI-native hardware, ClawCube delivers a curated lineup of OpenClaw-ready devices—from lightweight personal boxes to high-performance local AI systems.
